Our Story

Connection works better
in context.

Stringz started as a simple experiment — connecting strangers attending the same event through a group chat. When that experiment turned into a real relationship, it revealed something the dating industry had forgotten: the best connections happen through the things we already love doing.

While dating apps optimized for the swipe, we optimized for the encounter.

Stringz bridges the gap between digital discovery and real-life chemistry by connecting singles within the same communities, events, and environments.

We aren't replacing the real world — we're just making it easier to navigate.
